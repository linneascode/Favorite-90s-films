Reasoning

Why are these my favorite 90's movies? Well, it's a matter of personal taste, and, of course, my opinion is a tad superior, if you haven't noticed. All jokes aside, selecting my favorite movies from the 90s was a bit challenging for a couple of reasons. First, I was born in 1998, so I didn't experience the 90s firsthand. Second, I'm more inclined towards TV shows than movies. That's why, over on my IMDb profile, you'll find a link to my actual list of top shows and movies. Link for these are in the header.
I encourage you to create your own lists too, especially if you consume a lot of content like me. It's easy to forget your favorites over time. Imagine in 30 years, looking back and reminiscing about everything you've watched and rated. Peace out
